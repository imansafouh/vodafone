Why do we recycle...?

1. Oil

- Recycling used oil keeps it from polluting soil and water.
- Motor oil does not wear out—it just gets dirty—so recycling it saves a valuable resource.
- Less energy is required to produce a gallon of re-refined base stock than a base stock from crude oil.
- One gallon of used motor oil provides the same 2.5 quarts of lubricating oil as 42 gallons of crude oil.

2. Plastic

-Plastic is unsastainable.
-Plastic takes a long time to degrade & leaches toxins.
-Plastic harms wildlife.
-Plastic recycling conserves energy and the environment.

3. Aluminium

-Aluminium is an eco-friendly materials.
-Around 75% of all aluminium produced is still in use today.

4. Old Tires

-Prevent pollution caused by tire fires.
-Conserve landfill space.
-Creation of new products.
-Prevents spreading of diseases.

5. Electronics

-It benefits the economy.
-Supports the Community.
-It’s super easy to recycle e-waste.
